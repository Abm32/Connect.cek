About Us

Welcome to Connect.cek, your premier job portal connecting the vibrant community of the College of Engineering Karunagappally (CEK). Our platform is designed to foster connections between alumni, students, and faculty members, creating opportunities for networking, collaboration, and professional growth.

Our Mission

At Connect.cek, our mission is to empower individuals within the CEK community to achieve their career aspirations by providing a centralized platform for job opportunities, mentorship, and knowledge sharing.

What We Offer

Our platform offers a range of features including:

  • Job search and recruitment services
  • Networking opportunities with alumni and industry professionals
  • Access to career resources and development programs
  • Personalized job recommendations based on user preferences

Get Connected

Join Connect.cek today to take advantage of our extensive network and resources. Whether you're a recent graduate seeking employment, a current student exploring internship opportunities, or a seasoned professional looking to give back to the community, Connect.cek is here to support your journey.
